## ORGANIZATION

**Sun Glazing** was founded by **Mr. Madhusudhan a Mechanical Engineer** by profession, having 22 years of experience in the Aluminium industry. Sun Glazing is a leading Curtain wall Glazing Contractor in South India involved in the design, fabrication & [Installation](https://www.google.com/) of façade [envelopes](http://www.sunglazing.com/glass-contractors/), which includes Curtain wall Glazing, Unitized Glazing systems, Tension Cable systems & metal cladding. Structural Glazing

**Mr Madhusudhan**is a second generation entrepreneur started his carrier in business initially by setting up an[Aluminium](https://www.google.com/) Extrusion manufacturing Industry, in the year 1996. The company having a production capacity of about 3000 tons of Aluminium Profiles per annum, has been catering to various Industries such as Solar panels, Automobile, architectural, Electronics, manufacturing industry etc… Some of the customers are Eason reyrol, Tata BP Solar, Vero President Systems, Seimens, Alsthom etc.
