# About Us

**Sun Glazing** was founded by Mr. Madhusudhan a Mechanical Engineer by profession, having 22 years of experience in the Aluminium industry. Sun Glazing is a leading Curtain wall Glazing Contractor in South India involved in the design, fabrication & [Installation](https://www.google.com/) of façade [envelopes](http://www.sunglazing.com/glass-contractors/), which includes Curtain wall Glazing, Unitized Glazing systems, Tension Cable systems & metal cladding.

![Structural Glazing](https://www.sunglazing.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/02/Madhu-Photo.png)

### Mr. MADHUSUDHAN

Mr Madhusudhan is a second generation entrepreneur started his carrier in business initially by setting up an Aluminium Extrusion manufacturing Industry, in the year 1996. The company having a production capacity of about 3000 tons of Aluminium Profiles per annum, has been catering to various Industries such as Solar panels, Automobile, architectural, Electronics, manufacturing industry etc… Some of the customers are Eason reyrol, Tata BP Solar, Vero President Systems, Seimens, Alsthom etc.

![](https://www.sunglazing.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/Vishnu-Photo.png)

### Mr. VISHNU

Mr. Vishnu is associated with Sun Glazing since 2022 and oversees estimation, recruitment, project management and production management. He helped establish our new production facility, win new projects and change internal processes. He is chemical engineer from BITS Pilani Hyderabad campus, and MBA from ISB Hyderabad.

**In the year 2005, Mr Madhusudhan**had found huge business potential in the facade Glazing works, therefore the company diversified into design, Manufacture & Installation of Aluminium & Glass for building façade envelopes. Initially the company was manufacturing complete Unitized Glazing [Panels](https://www.google.com/) at its factory as an ancillary for a multinational Company for various projects such as the [Hyderabad](http://www.sunglazing.com/glass-contractors/) International Airport, Ascendas IT Park, Novotel, Deloitte etc.

Thereon from the year 2008 he has moved the company for forward integration & has been taking up turnkey Façade Glazing contracts and executed various works such as Unitized Glazing, Bullet proof Glazing, Kinetic facade, Metal Cladding, Tension Cable networks etc. Henceforth the companyearned a reputation as one of the reliable brands in Facade Glazing Industry in South India.
